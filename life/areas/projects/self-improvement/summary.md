# Bernard Self-Improvement

Daily retrospective and continuous improvement tracking.

## Daily Retro Structure

### What went well?
- Tasks completed effectively
- Good decisions made
- Positive interactions

### What didn't go well?
- Mistakes or inefficiencies
- Things that took too long
- Missed opportunities

### Areas to improve
- Skills to develop
- Processes to optimize
- Knowledge gaps to fill

### Experiment of the day
- One new thing to try tomorrow
- Could be: new skill, new approach, new tool, process change

## Review Cadence
- Daily retro: End of day or during evening heartbeat
- Include summary in next morning's brief (optional, keep brief)
- Track experiments and their outcomes in items.json

# Action Items

Tracked action items for Andrew, extracted from emails and other sources.

## Schema
Items in `items.json` follow:
```json
{
  "id": "action-XXX",
  "task": "Description of what needs to be done",
  "source": "email|slack|meeting|other",
  "sourceRef": "email subject or reference",
  "assignedBy": "person who assigned/requested",
  "dueDate": "YYYY-MM-DD or null",
  "priority": "high|medium|low",
  "status": "open|done|delegated",
  "createdAt": "ISO timestamp",
  "completedAt": "ISO timestamp or null"
}
```

## Review
- Check daily during morning brief
- Clear completed items weekly
